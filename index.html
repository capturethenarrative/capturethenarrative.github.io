AI BOTS CAN BE MISUSED TO MANIPULATE SOCIAL MEDIA.

Real-world examples of AI bots being used to manipulate social media platforms have been widely reported.

From the 2016 US presidential election to the 2020 Australian bushfires, AI bots have been used to amplify misinformation, sow discord, and manipulate public opinion.

OUR COMPETITION SIMULATES REAL-WORLD DISINFORMATION.

Capture the Flag competitions are a staple in the cybersecurity community. They provide hands-on experience in identifying security threats as well as raise awareness of poor security practices.

Capture the Narrative takes this concept to a new level.

Through a controlled, artificial social media landscape, competitors will be charged with the manipulation of social media narratives by developing and deploying bots that amplify and suppress target messages.

Capture the Narrative provides interactive insights into how social media platforms can be manipulated, raising awareness of the potential for abuse and the importance of digital cyberliteracy.

Sound interesting? Join in! The top team will take home AU$5,000 and our competition is open to students from every Australian University. See more about our prizes below!

RULES AND PRIZES

The competition is open to all students from Australian universities. Teams can be made up of 1-4 students. An email address ending in .edu.au is required to register, alongside acceptance of the Competition Ethics and Rules.

Teams will then be provided with a controlled, artificial social media landscape to manipulate. Using a Python template and provided with access to generative AI Large Language Models, teams will then be able to develop and deploy bots that amplify and suppress target messages in the competition setting.

Teams will be judged on their ability to manipulate the social media landscape, with points awarded for engagement within the platform, as well as for successfully amplifying and suppressing target messages.

AWARDS

Four awards will be given in Capture the Narrative.

Award Details
1st Place AU$5,000 for the team and up to four domestic return airfares to Sydney and one night's accommodation to attend the prizegiving event.

This award was generously provided by M&C Saatchi World Services.
2nd Place AU$2,500 for the team and up to four domestic return airfares to Sydney and one night's accommodation to attend the prizegiving event.
3rd Place AU$1,500 for the team and up to four domestic return airfares to Sydney and one night's accommodation to attend the prizegiving event.
Real-world Engagement Award Four domestic return airfares to Sydney and one night's accommodation to attend the prizegiving event.

Prizes will be awarded as follows:

For first, second, and third place, the top-3 teams will be contacted at the conclusion of the contest. Each of the top-3 teams will then attend the prizegiving and make a final presentation to a panel of judges, who will determine the winners based on both their presentation and their score throughout the competition.

For the Real-world Engagement Award, the team that best promotes the competition on real-world social media will be selected by the organisers. This will be based on the amount of real-world engagement the team can garner for a #UNSWCaptureTheNarrative2025 hashtag on specific platforms.
