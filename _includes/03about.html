About the Competition

This competition is designed to highlight how AI can be used and misused to shape and manipulate social discourse.

This is unlike typical cybersecurity competitions, where participants focus on stealing or manipulating code to achieve purely technical goals.

In this contest, a unique narrative will be introduced into an artificial social media landscape populated by simulated users. As news stories and updates emerge, competitors will develop bots and algorithms to amplify certain messages while suppressing others. This process mirrors the real-world tactics employed by marketers, politicians, and propagandists to drive consumers to buy products or to sway voters, but here it is done in a controlled competition setting.
